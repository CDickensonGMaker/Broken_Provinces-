================================================================================
PHILOSOPHY & LORE
================================================================================

Morthane is NOT evil. The deity represents:
  • Death as a natural part of the cycle
  • Rebirth as transformation and renewal
  • Opposition to undeath (zombies, skeletons, liches)
  • Honoring the dead and protecting burial grounds

Devotees are:
  • Philosophical undead hunters
  • Guardians of the cycle
  • Speakers for the dead
  • Champions against corruption

This makes them morally GOOD characters who understand mortality,
NOT dark cultists or evil necromancers.
