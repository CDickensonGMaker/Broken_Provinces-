GAELA QUEST CHAIN - QUICK REFERENCE
Temple of Three Gods - Goddess of Harvest
================================================================================

QUEST GIVER: Priestess Elara (priest_gaela_dalhurst) in Dalhurst Temple

TOTAL QUESTS: 13
- Main Chain: 10 quests
- Bonus Quests: 2
- Repeatable: 1 (weekly)

TOTAL REWARDS (Main Chain):
- Gold: 2,725g
- XP: 8,150 XP
- Faction: +395 church_of_gaela, +285 church_of_three

================================================================================
MAIN QUEST CHAIN
================================================================================

TIER 1: INITIATE (Quests 1-3)
------------------------------
[1] Seeds of Faith (gaela_01_first_offering)
    - Bring offering, help farmer
    - 50g, 200 XP

[2] The Healer's Garden (gaela_02_healing_herbs)
    - Gather herbs from 3 locations
    - 75g, 300 XP

[3] Guardians of the Field (gaela_03_protect_harvest)
    - Kill dire wolves and giant rats threatening Millbrook
    - 100g, 400 XP

TIER 2: ACOLYTE (Quests 4-5)
-----------------------------
[4] The Sacred Grove (gaela_04_sacred_grove)
    - Negotiate nature vs economy conflict
    - CHOICE-BASED QUEST
    - 150g, 500 XP

[5] The Mother's Embrace (gaela_05_devotion_choice)
    - DEVOTION CHOICE: Accept or decline becoming a Devotee
    - SETS FLAG: gaela_devotee (required for quests 6-10)
    - 200g, 600 XP

TIER 3: DEVOTEE EXCLUSIVE (Quests 6-8)
---------------------------------------
PREREQUISITE: gaela_devotee flag REQUIRED

[6] Root of Corruption (gaela_06_blight_source)
    - Stop blight spreading from Willow Dale
    - 250g, 750 XP

[7] Voice of the Green (gaela_07_spirit_of_land)
    - Free trapped land spirit near Crossroads
    - 300g, 900 XP

[8] The Eternal Seed (gaela_08_seed_of_life)
    - Retrieve legendary Seed to cure plague
    - Defeat Ancient Treant
    - 350g, 1000 XP

TIER 4: CHAMPION (Quests 9-10)
-------------------------------
PREREQUISITE: gaela_devotee flag REQUIRED

[9] When Harvests Fail (gaela_09_famine_threat)
    - Stop regional famine (multi-location)
    - Defeat Dark Ritualist
    - 500g, 1500 XP

[10] The Lifebringer (gaela_10_lifebringer)
     - FINAL QUEST: Become Gaela's Champion
     - Perform ritual to restore corrupted lands
     - SETS FLAGS: gaela_champion, lifebringer_ritual_complete
     - 750g, 2000 XP
     - REWARDS: Gaela's Verdant Robes, Champion's Laurel, Eternal Harvest Ring

BONUS & REPEATABLE QUESTS

- Prerequisite: gaela_devotee

- Escort mission
- Prerequisite: Quest 3 complete

================================================================================
KEY FEATURES
================================================================================

DEVOTEE SYSTEM:
- Quest 5 offers devotion choice (accept or decline)
- Accepting sets flag: gaela_devotee
- Unlocks quests 6-10 (exclusive content)
- Does NOT lock out other temple quests
- Other priests acknowledge devotion in dialogue

CHOICE-BASED QUESTS:
- Quest 4: Three outcomes (protect grove, allow logging, find alternative)
- Quest 10: Accept or decline Champion title

FACTION INTEGRATION:
- Primary: church_of_gaela
- Secondary: church_of_three (parent faction)
- Regional: millbrook_farmers, thornfield_citizens, dalhurst_merchants

GEOGRAPHIC LIMITS:
- All quests in: Elder Moor, Dalhurst, Thornfield, Millbrook, Crossroads,
  Willow Dale, Bandit Hideout, Kazer-Dun Entrance
- DO NOT send players south of Kazer-Dun

PROGRESSION PATH
================================================================================

1. Talk to Priestess Elara in Dalhurst Temple
2. Complete Quests 1-3 (Initiate tier)
3. Complete Quest 4 (choice-based)
4. Quest 5: DEVOTION CHOICE
   - Accept → Unlock quests 6-10
   - Decline → Chain ends
5. Complete Quests 6-8 (Devotee tier)
6. Complete Quests 9-10 (Champion tier)
7. Optional: Bonus quests and weekly repeatable

ESTIMATED COMPLETION TIME: 8-12 hours for full chain
